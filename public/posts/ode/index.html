Exam 3 question 1) Solve separable differential equation $y’=y^2cos(x)$ , with the boundary condition $y(0)=1$.

$$y’=y^2cos(x)$$ $$\frac{dy}{y^2}=cos(x)dx$$ $$-\frac{1}{y}=sin(x)$$ $$y=\frac{1}{c-sin(x)}$$ solve for boundary $y(0)=1$ $$1=\frac{1}{c-sin(0)}$$ $$1=\frac{1}{c-0}$$ $$c=1$$ place into re-arranged $$y=\frac{1}{1-sin(x)}$$
